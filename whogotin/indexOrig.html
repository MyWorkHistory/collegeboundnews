Who Got In? 2006
COLLEGE BOUND's National Survey of College Admissions Trends
20th Annual Edition

Each September, COLLEGE BOUND surveys a representative sample of colleges and universities to see how their previous admissions season developed and what admissions numbers they collected. The 2006 book edition of WHO GOT IN? features admissions data and trends from representative public and private colleges and universities from coast to coast.
