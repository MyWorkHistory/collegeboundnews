COLLEGE BOUND and collegeboundnews.com deliver readers inside information from admissions offices across the U.S. on their latest admissions decisions and trends; on what they are looking for in students next year; on where they are looking for students from out-of-state; on numbers of applications they receive each season; on early admissions trends; retention rates; minority recruitment; test scores and high school rank of admitted students; on financial aid opportunities; on new scholarships and academic programs; and much more ...

This is the kind of month-to-month information that you can't find in most guide books because things change before they are even off the press. But COLLEGE BOUND and collegeboundnews.com, not only give you the information quickly, but you get the inside scoop from those in the know.
